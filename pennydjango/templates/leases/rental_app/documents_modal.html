Documents Information
PROOF OF INCOME CHECKLIST

In addition to your rental application, we will need a selection of the following documents. Please note: providing ample documentation improves your chance of getting this apartment.

WORKING PROFESSIONAL
  • Three recent paychecks
  • Last year’s tax return (W2 form)
  • Letter of employment stating salary
  • Recent bank statements
  • US guarantor
  • Letter from landlord and/or copies of recent rent checks

FREELANCER OR “OFF THE BOOKS” EMPLOYEE
  • Bank statements showing recent deposits
  • Letter of employment stating payments
  • Freelance paychecks
  • Last year’s tax return (1099 form)
  • US guarantor
  • Letter from landlord and/or copies of recent rent checks

STUDENT
  • Student loan documentation showing housing allowance
  • Recent bank statements
  • Recent paychecks
  • Last year’s tax return (W2, 1099, 1040, etc.)
  • US guarantor
  • Letter from landlord and/or copies of recent rent checks

GUARANTOR
  • Proof of income (paychecks, letter of employment, etc.)
  • Last year’s tax returns (W2, 1099, 1040, etc.)
  • Recent bank statements
